Our Story

Who We Are

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-led nonprofit dedicated to uplifting India's marginalized children. We believe that access to quality education, mentorship, and a nurturing community can transform lives across generations.

Started by a group of passionate college students in 2017, our grassroots movement has grown into a network spanning 48 cities — powered entirely by volunteers who share one vision: a just and educated India.

Our Programs

From classrooms to career guidance, our initiatives address every stage of a child's growth journey.

🎒
Shiksha Abhiyan

Our flagship education drive delivers free tutoring, school supplies, and digital literacy to government-school students in underserved communities.

Education
💡
Career Compass

One-on-one mentoring sessions connect students with professionals across industries, opening doors that were previously out of reach.

Mentorship
🎨
Kalakari — Art for All

Creative expression workshops in music, visual arts, and theatre help children discover confidence and emotional intelligence beyond academics.

Creativity
🏥
Swasthya Camps

Free health check-up drives and hygiene awareness workshops keep our communities strong and children ready to learn.

Health

Digital Disha

Bridging the digital divide through computer labs, coding bootcamps, and internet access programs for first-gen learners.

Technology
